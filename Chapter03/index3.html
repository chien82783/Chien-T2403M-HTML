Scrambled eggs

eggs are one of my favorite foods. here is a recipe for deliciously rich scambled aggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs crea

method

  1. melt butte in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate umtil served)
